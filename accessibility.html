Accessibility Statement

ThaBarWa International Foundation is committed to making this landing page usable for as many people as possible, including people with disabilities.

Standards and goals

We aim to meet or exceed:

  • WCAG 2.1 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) at Level AA, and Level AAA where practical.
  • Relevant aspects of ISO 40500 (equivalent to WCAG 2.0) and best practice for international NGOs.

What we do

  • Semantic HTML (headings, landmarks, sections) so assistive technologies can navigate meaningfully.
  • Skip link to jump to main content.
  • Visible focus styles for keyboard users (outline and offset).
  • Sufficient colour contrast between text and background (meeting WCAG AA).
  • Responsive layout that works at different zoom levels and screen sizes.
  • Support for reduced motion (respects prefers-reduced-motion).
  • Links that open in a new window are indicated in text and with rel="noopener noreferrer".
