About ThaBarWa

The Foundation is built on the Buddha’s teaching of Generosity, Non-harming, and Mental Development — the solid platform for every “house” of good deeds.

The ThaBarWa House is a Good Deeds House on this platform, built with Wisdom as the roof, Concentration as the floor, and Morality as the walls. From project to project, land to land, country to country, we serve rich and poor, young and old, healthy and sick.

  • Free stay and food for everyone, without limit
  • Free meditation, mindfulness and detachment
  • Freedom and transformation of life
  • Monks, nuns and volunteers working for good deeds
  • 100% of donations go to the chosen cause or project
