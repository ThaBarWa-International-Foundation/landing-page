Mission & Vision

Give, Share, Care, Protect & Co-operate. Our aim is to create opportunities for individuals to perform good deeds on a consistent basis – the Foundation is the Buddha's teaching of Generosity, Non-harming, and Mental Development.

Mission

Vision

The Thabarwa House: a Good Deeds House on the platform of Buddhism – built with Wisdom as the roof, Concentration as the floor, Morality as the walling.

Sayadaw Ashin Ottamathara

Master Sayadaw Ashin Ottamathara founded the ThaBarWa Centre with the vision and philosophy of Generosity, Non-harming, and Mental Development. The Foundation creates opportunities for individuals to perform a variety of good deeds on a consistent basis – free stay and food, free meditation and mindfulness, and monks, nuns and volunteers working for good deeds worldwide.
